About Us:

Tubonge nawe is a website created by Mary Watiri, with the goal of making online learning accessible to all, regardless of ability or language barriers.
Mary is passionate about education and accessibility, and believes that everyone deserves equal access to educational resources.
The name Tubonge nawe means "we are together" in Swahili, and reflects the website's commitment to inclusion and community.
